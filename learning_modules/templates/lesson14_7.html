Time for the seventh and final mode.

Locrian Mode

Locrian Mode: 1 - b2 - b3 - 4 - b5 - b6 - b7
Unique Notes: 1 - b3 - b5 (explained below)



So first off, why 3 unique notes?
The reason why we choose those three is because they form a diminished triad.
None of the other modes have a diminished triad.
We will now dive into two patterns but not the cumulative pattern since due to how
I setup the cumulative pattern's aeolian mode has the locrian mode just like the phrygian/lydian
situation.
