Notes On Your Guitar

The Open Strings On A Standard Tuned Guitar

If you just bought a guitar, chances are that it is tuned to the standard EADGBE tuning.
Here is an image to help you orient yourself on the guitar.

The last E after B is the High E string and when numbered is the first.
It is common practice to know these open strings by numbers.
1 = High E
2 = B
3 = G
4 = D
5 = A
6 = Low E
This can be confusing at first but with time and practice it will become easier.
Now that you understand the orientation of the strings, you are more than welcome to search
"mnemonic acronyms for guitar strings", this should yield plenty results, memorize it and remember it.
