Tuning Up!

Now that you know the names of the open strings on your guitar, it is time to see if you are in tune
If you have a tuner at home feel free to use it, if not, go to the home page and click on the tile that says "tuner".
Now start from your sixth string == low E string. Pluck it and let it ring while the tuner is one.
The following table shows you the frequencies you want to hit for each string.

String # Note Frequency Scientific Name
6 E 82.41 Hz E2
5 A 110.00 Hz A2
4 D 146.83 Hz D3
3 G 196.00 Hz G3
2 B 246.94 Hz B3
1 E 329.63 E4
